Based on your responses — test request summary
Lynch syndrome referral — St Mark's Hospital Family Cancer Clinic Any confirmed or suspected Lynch syndrome should be referred to the St Mark's Hospital Family Cancer Clinic (SMCFIC).

Referral criteria (Modified Amsterdam Criteria):
  • ≥3 cases of Lynch-related cancer over ≥2 generations
  • ≥1 case diagnosed < 50 years

Lynch-related cancers: Colorectal, endometrial, epithelial ovarian, endocervical, gastric, oesophageal junctional, small bowel, cholangiocarcinoma, pancreatic, ureteric, transitional cell of renal pelvis, prostate, glioblastoma, multiple sebaceous tumours.

Contact SMCFIC: LNWH-tr.SMCFIC@nhs.net
